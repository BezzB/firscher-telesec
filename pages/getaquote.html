Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our consultation process

How long does it take to receive a quote?

After submitting your request, you can expect to receive an initial response within 24-48 business hours. For complex projects, a detailed quote may take 3-5 business days following our initial consultation.

Is the consultation free?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to understand your needs and provide a tailored quote. There's no obligation to proceed with our services after receiving the quote.

What information should I prepare for the consultation?

It's helpful to have an overview of your current infrastructure, specific challenges you're facing, business goals, timeline expectations, and budget considerations. The more information you can provide, the more accurate our quote will be.

Can you work with specific budget constraints?

Yes, we understand that businesses have budget constraints. During the consultation process, we'll work with you to prioritize needs and develop solutions that provide the best value within your budget parameters.

What happens after I accept the quote?

Once you accept the quote, we'll prepare a detailed service agreement outlining the scope, deliverables, timeline, and payment terms. After signing the agreement, we'll schedule a project kickoff meeting to begin implementation.
