Our Story

Founded in 2013, Fischer Telesec emerged from a vision to bridge the technology gap for businesses in East Africa. What began as a small team of passionate tech experts has grown into a leading telecommunications and IT solutions provider, serving a diverse range of clients across multiple industries.

Our journey has been defined by a commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. From our humble beginnings in Mombasa, we've expanded our reach across Kenya and East Africa, building a reputation for excellence in enterprise technology solutions.

Today, we pride ourselves on delivering cutting-edge telecommunications and IT infrastructure that empowers businesses to thrive in the digital era.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with reliable, innovative, and scalable technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and competitive advantage in an increasingly digital world.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner for businesses across East Africa, recognized for our expertise, innovation, and unwavering commitment to client success.

Our Journey

A decade of growth, innovation, and impact

2013

Fischer Telesec founded in Mombasa, Kenya with a focus on network infrastructure services.

2015

Expanded service offerings to include IT infrastructure and support services. Partnered with major hardware and software providers.

2017

Launched data center services division. Completed first major government infrastructure project.

2019

Expanded operations to Tanzania and Uganda. Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification for quality management.

2021

Introduced cloud services and cybersecurity solutions. Celebrated completion of 300+ successful projects.

Today

Serving 500+ clients across East Africa with a comprehensive suite of telecommunications and IT solutions.
