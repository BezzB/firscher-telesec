Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve businesses across East Africa, with a strong presence in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. Our headquarters is in Mombasa, Kenya, but we work with clients throughout the region.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

For our clients with service agreements, we offer response times ranging from 1-4 hours depending on the severity of the issue. Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical systems.

Do you offer service level agreements (SLAs)?

Yes, we offer customizable SLAs that can be tailored to your specific business needs. Our SLAs include guaranteed response times, regular maintenance, and proactive monitoring.

What is your process for new client engagements?

We typically begin with a consultation to understand your business needs, followed by an assessment of your current infrastructure. We then develop a proposal with recommendations and pricing. Upon approval, we create a detailed implementation plan.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Absolutely. We can collaborate with your internal IT staff, providing specialized expertise, additional resources, or specific services that complement your existing capabilities.
